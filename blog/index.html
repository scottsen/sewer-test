Sewer Line Health

July 1, 2024

Are you concerned about the health of your sewer line? Here are 5 signs that might indicate a problem: Gurgling sounds from drains Slow draining in multiple fixtures Sewage odors inside or outside your home Wet spots in your yard Mold growth on walls or ceilings If you notice any of these signs, it’s time to call a professional for an inspection.
